Obtaining a Visa

The Complete Guide to Obtaining a Visa for Tanzania

Tanzania, an East African gem known for its diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes, welcomes visitors from all over the world. To explore this vibrant nation, understanding the visa requirements and procedures is crucial. This comprehensive guide walks you through the different visa types available for Tanzania and the steps to obtain one.

Tourist Visa Application Process

1. Research and gather documents: Start by visiting the Tanzanian embassy or consulate in your country or consult their official website. Ensure you have a valid passport, a completed application form, a recent passport-sized photograph, and proof of return or onward flight.

2. Submit your application: Fill out the application form accurately and attach the required documents. Submit your application either in person or by mail, adhering to the guidelines provided by the Tanzanian embassy or consulate. Check online Visa application

3. Pay the visa fee: Review the current visa fee structure, as it may vary based on your citizenship and the duration of your stay. Pay the fee by following the embassy’s instructions, such as submitting a bank draft or paying online. Check visa fees

4. Await processing: Depending on the embassy’s workload, the processing time may vary. It is advisable to apply well in advance to allow ample time for visa processing. Consider contacting the embassy or consulate to check the status of your application, if required.

Visa on Arrival

For some nationalities, Tanzania offers the option of obtaining a Visa on Arrival. This process allows you to apply for your visa upon arrival at the airport or other designated entry points. However, it is essential to ensure that your nationality qualifies for this option, as well as familiarize yourself with the current requirements and fees. Check visa application guidelines

  • Familiarize yourself with the requirements: Check the official Tanzanian government websites or consult with your travel agent to ensure your nationality is eligible for Visa on Arrival. Take note of any specific requirements, such as the need for a Yellow Fever vaccination certificate.
  • Arrive and complete the application: Upon arrival at the airport in Tanzania, locate the Visa on Arrival counter. Fill out the application form available, providing accurate information such as your purpose of visit, duration of stay, and contact details.
  • Pay the visa fee: Pay the visa fee in the provided currency, either in cash or card, as per the guidelines at the airport.
  • Receive your visa: Once your application and payment are processed, you will receive your visa. Ensure all the details are correct before proceeding through immigration.

Other Considerations

Extensions

If you wish to extend your stay while visiting Tanzania, contact the Immigration Department for the necessary information and procedures to obtain an extension.

Multiple-entry visas

If you plan to leave Tanzania and re-enter during your stay, consider applying for a Multiple-entry visa to avoid any inconvenience.

Visa requirements for minors

Families traveling with minors should review the specific requirements for child visas and seek clarity from the Tanzanian embassy or consulate regarding any additional documentations.

Now armed with the knowledge of visa requirements and procedures, you are ready to embark on your Tanzanian adventure hassle-free. Remember to plan your trip well in advance, ensuring compliance with all necessary regulations to enjoy your time in Tanzania to the fullest. Safe travels!
