ABOUT MIGRATION SAFARIS

Witness the Greatest Wildlife Spectacle on Earth

A Migration Safari offers a front-row seat to one of nature's most breathtaking events - the Great Wildebeest Migration. Every year, more than two million wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles embark on an incredible journey across the vast plains of the Serengeti ecosystem in search of fresh grazing lands and water.

This remarkable movement is driven by seasonal rainfall and follows an ancient natural cycle that has taken place for thousands of years. Along the way, the herds face numerous challenges, including dangerous river crossings, hungry predators, and changing weather conditions, creating some of the most dramatic wildlife encounters in Africa.

The Great Migration is often described as the "Greatest Wildlife Show on Earth" and is considered one of the Seven Natural Wonders of Africa. It is a truly unforgettable experience that attracts travelers, photographers, filmmakers, and wildlife enthusiasts from around the world.

An Ever-Changing Adventure

Unlike a traditional safari, the migration is constantly moving. Depending on the time of year, visitors can witness different stages of this incredible journey - from the calving season in the southern Serengeti, where thousands of newborn wildebeest take their first steps, to the dramatic river crossings in the northern Serengeti, where massive herds brave crocodile-filled waters.

Each season offers a unique perspective and unforgettable wildlife experiences.

PLANNING CALENDAR

Best Time to Experience the Migration

The Great Migration is a year-round cycle driven by seasonal rainfall. Use our calendar to align your safari goals with the herds' location.

Jan - Mar

Calving Season

The southern Serengeti becomes a nursery as hundreds of thousands of wildebeest give birth within a short period.

Best For:

  • Newborn wildlife
  • Predator activity
  • Photography
Apr - Jun

The Journey Begins

The herds begin moving northward through the central Serengeti.

Best For:

  • Massive herds
  • Fewer crowds
  • Scenic landscapes
Jul - Oct

River Crossings

One of the most famous stages of the migration as animals cross dangerous rivers.

Best For:

  • Mara River crossings
  • Dramatic wildlife action
  • Bucket-list experiences
Nov - Dec

Return Journey

The herds move back toward the southern Serengeti following seasonal rains.

Best For:

  • Wildlife viewing
  • Green landscapes
  • Excellent game drives
