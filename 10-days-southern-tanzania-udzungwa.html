10-Days Southern Tanzania Safari

& Udzungwa Trek

Embark on the adventure of a lifetime with our Epic 10-Days Southern Tanzania Safari & Udzungwa Mountain Trek! This incredible journey will take you through some of the most breathtaking and untouched wilderness in Tanzania.

Witness the stunning beauty of the Selous Game Reserve, one of the largest game reserves in Africa, and home to a diverse array of wildlife including elephants, lions, and hippos. Explore the remote and untouched beauty of Ruaha National Park, known for its large population of elephants and baobab trees.

But the adventure doesn’t stop there! Trek through the lush forests of the Udzungwa Mountains, known as the “Galapagos of Africa” for their unique biodiversity and stunning landscapes. Get up close and personal with rare species of primates, birds, and plants found nowhere else on earth.

Our expert guides will lead you on unforgettable game drives, bush walks, and mountain treks, ensuring you have the best possible experience in this remote and unspoiled corner of Tanzania.

Don’t miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to explore southern Tanzania in all its wild beauty. Book your Epic 10-Days Southern Tanzania Safari & Udzungwa Mountain Trek today and create memories that will last a lifetime!

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dar es Salaam
Welcome to Tanzania! Upon arrival at Julius Nyerere International Airport in Dar es Salaam, you will be transferred to your accommodation at Hotel Slipway. Take some time to relax and settle in after your journey. If time allows, you can explore the nearby Slipway area with its shops, restaurants, and beautiful views of the Indian Ocean.

Day 2: Dar es salaam to Nyerere
After breakfast, depart for Nyerere National Park. Check into Selous Kinga Lodge and enjoy an evening boat safari.

Day 3: Nyerere National Park
Full-day game drive in Nyerere National Park, discovering lakes, rivers, and diverse wildlife including wild dogs, elephants, and lions.

Day 4: Nyerere to Mikumi
Depart Nyerere and travel to Mikumi National Park, arriving in time for an evening game drive and dinner at Mikumi Wildlife Camp.

Day 5: Udzungwa National park
Hike through the lush rainforest of Udzungwa Mountains National Park, visiting Sanje Waterfalls and searching for endemic monkey species.

Day 6: Mikumi-Dar es Salaam
Morning game drive in Mikumi, then travel uphill to the historic town of Dar es Salaam for lunch and sightseeing.

Day 7: Ruaha National Park
Enter Ruaha National Park and begin your search for predators along the Great Ruaha River. Overnight at Ruaha River Lodge.

Day 8: Ruaha National park
Spend another full day experiencing the wild beauty and exceptional game viewing of Ruaha. Overnight at Ruaha River Lodge.

Day 9: Fly to Dar es Salaam
Board a scheduled flight from Ruaha airstrip back to Dar es Salaam, checking in at Hotel Slipway.

Day 10: Departure
Transfer to Julius Nyerere Airport for your flight back home.
