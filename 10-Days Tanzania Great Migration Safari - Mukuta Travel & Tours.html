10-Days Tanzania Great Migration Safari

View all photos

Escape to the stunning landscapes of Tanzania and experience the awe-inspiring Great Migration with our 10-Day Great Migration Safari. This luxury safari will take you deep into the heart of the Serengeti, where you’ll witness one of nature’s most incredible spectacles.

From the comfort of our exclusive lodges and luxury tented camps, you’ll have front-row seats to the Great Migration as millions of wildebeest, zebras, and gazelles make their annual journey across the Serengeti in search of greener pastures. This dramatic display of nature’s raw power and beauty is a once-in-a-lifetime experience that will leave you breathless.

.

Day 1: Arusha-Arrival

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro Airport, you will be transferred to Arusha. Check into Arusha Coffee Lodge and spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the lodge’s coffee plantation or relaxing by the pool.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

After breakfast, you will depart for Tarangire National Park. Enjoy a full-day game drive in Tarangire, known for its large herds of elephants and baobab trees. In the late afternoon, check into Lake Burunge Tented Camp for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Tarangire National Park

Spend the morning in Tarangire National Park, exploring more of its wildlife and landscapes. In the afternoon, drive to Ngorongoro Farm House, a charming lodge located near the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Enjoy the evening at leisure, with the option to take a guided walk around the lodge.

Day 4: Central Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, drive to Central Serengeti National Park. Enjoy a game drive en route to the lodge, where you will spend the next two nights. Explore the vast plains of the Serengeti and spot a variety of wildlife, including the famous wildebeest migration (seasonal). Over Kubu Kubu Tented Lodge

Day 5: Central Serengeti National Park

Spend the day in Central Serengeti National Park, continuing your exploration of this iconic wildlife destination. Take game drives in search of the Big Five and witness the predator-prey interactions that make the Serengeti so unique.

Day 6: Northern Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, drive to the Northern Serengeti National Park. This area is known for its river crossings during the wildebeest migration (seasonal). Enjoy a game drive en route to Mara Mara Tented Lodge, where you will spend the next three nights.

Day 7: Northern Serengeti National Park

Spend the day in the Northern Serengeti, exploring its diverse landscapes and abundant wildlife. Witness the incredible spectacle of the wildebeest crossing the Mara River, as well as other predators and their prey.  Dinner and overnight at Mara Mara Tented Lodge

 

Day 9: Fly from Northern Serengeti -Arusha

Morning game drives in the northern Serengeti and later transfer to the Kogatende Airstrip for your flight to Arusha. Dinner and overnight at Gran Melia Arusha

Day 10: Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Kilimanjaro Airport for your departure. End of the safari.
