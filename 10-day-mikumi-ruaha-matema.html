TRIP OVERVIEW

Embark on a journey through Tanzania's untamed Southern Circuit. From the wildlife-rich plains of Mikumi to the rugged wilderness of Ruaha, ending with the pristine shores of Matema Beach on Lake Nyasa. This is more than a safari; it's an immersion into the heart of Africa.

DAY-BY-DAY ITINERARY

Day 1: Arrival & Dar es Salaam

Welcome to Tanzania! Transfer from Julius Nyerere International Airport to your hotel.

  • Airport pickup and transfer
  • Hotel check-in at Hotel Slipway
  • Evening briefing and dinner

Day 2-3: Mikumi National Park

Drive to Mikumi for an intensive wildlife experience. Known as "Little Serengeti," it offers incredible sightings.

  • Morning drive through picturesque landscapes
  • Afternoon game drives
  • Elephant and Lion sightings
  • Overnight at Vuma Hills Tented Camp

Day 4-6: Ruaha National Park

Head to the rugged Ruaha, Tanzania's largest park, famous for its massive elephant herds and predators.

  • Walking Safaris
  • Sunset over the Great Ruaha River
  • Leopard and Wild Dog tracking

Day 7-10: Matema Beach & Departure

Relax at Lake Nyasa's Matema Beach before heading back to Mbeya for your flight.

  • Lakeside relaxation
  • Local cultural tours
  • Return to Dar es Salaam
