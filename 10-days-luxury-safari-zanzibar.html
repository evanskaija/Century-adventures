Overview

Embark on an incredible journey through Tanzania's iconic national parks, where you will have the opportunity to witness the famous Big Five – lion, leopard, elephant, rhino, and buffalo.

Our expert guides will navigate you through Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Crater, Tarangire National Park, and Lake Manyara National Park, ensuring you have the best chance of spotting these magnificent creatures up close.

After your exhilarating wildlife encounters, it's time to relax and unwind on the pristine beaches of Zanzibar. Known for its turquoise waters, palm-fringed shores, and vibrant coral reefs, Zanzibar offers the perfect setting for a blissful beach getaway. Spend your days lounging on white sandy beaches, swimming in crystal-clear waters, and indulging in delicious seafood delicacies.

Itinerary

Day 1
Arrival in Arusha
Arrive in Arusha and check in to the luxurious Gran Melia Arusha. Enjoy a welcome dinner and relax after your journey.
Day 2
Tarangire National Park
After breakfast, road transfer to Tarangire National Park. Enjoy an exciting game drive and overnight at Maramboi Tented Lodge.
Day 3
Ngorongoro Crater
Descend into the Ngorongoro Crater for a spectacular game drive. Overnight at Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge.
Day 4
Central Serengeti National Park
Drive to Serengeti National Park. Enjoy game drives en route and overnight at a luxury lodge in Central Serengeti.
Days 5 – 6
Central Serengeti National Park
Spend two full days exploring the Central Serengeti. Witness abundant wildlife and breathtaking landscapes.
Day 8
Fly from Central Serengeti to Zanzibar
Morning game drive and transfer to the airstrip for your flight to Zanzibar. Transfer to your beach resort.
Days 9 – 10
Beach Time
Relax and enjoy the white sandy beaches and turquoise waters of Zanzibar. Optional activities available.
Day 11
Departure
Transfer to the airport for your departure flight.
