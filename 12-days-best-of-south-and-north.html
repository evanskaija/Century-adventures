12 Days Best of South and North

Ruaha, Tarangire, Serengeti & Ngorongoro

Embark on an unforgettable 12-day adventure to witness the breathtaking beauty of South and Northern Tanzania on safari. This once-in-a-lifetime experience will take you through some of the most iconic national parks and conservation areas in the region, including Ruaha, Tarangire, Serengeti, and Ngorongoro.

In Ruaha National Park, you’ll have the opportunity to witness diverse wildlife in a pristine and remote setting. Keep your eyes peeled for lions, elephants, giraffes, and more as you explore this hidden gem of a park.

Next, head to Tarangire National Park, known for its large herds of elephants and iconic baobab trees. Enjoy game drives through the park as you search for leopards, cheetahs, and a variety of bird species.

The Serengeti is up next, where you’ll witness the Great Migration of wildebeest and zebras as they make their way across the vast savannah. Experience the thrill of seeing these animals up close in their natural habitat.

No trip to Tanzania would be complete without a visit to the Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best places in Africa to see the Big Five. Descend into the crater and witness a truly unique ecosystem.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dar es Salaam
Arrive in Dar es Salaam, transfer by road to Hotel Blue Sapphire for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2 : Fly to Dar es Salaam
Fly from Dar es Salaam to Dar es Salaam town, upon arrival Transfer by road to Ruaha National Park, and enjoy lunch and evening game viewing until sunset at Ruaha River Lodge.

Day 3: Ruaha National park
Full day game drive in Ruaha National Park, followed by a night game drive. Dinner and overnight at Ruaha River Lodge.

Day 4: Ruaha National Park to Dar es Salaam
Early morning game drive and later Transfer by road to Dar es Salaam town, check in at Dar es Salaam Sunset Hotel for lunch, dinner, and overnight stay.

Day 5: Tarangire National park
Transfer by road to Tarangire National Park, dinner, and overnight at Tarangire Simba Lodge.

Day 6: Tarangire National park
Full-day game drive in Tarangire National Park with packed lunch. Dinner and overnight at Tarangire Simba Lodge.

Day 7: Tarangire to Karatu
Enjoy a walking safari with Rangers in Tarangire National Park, then transfer by road to Baghayo Garden Suites for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 8: Serengeti National park
Transfer by road to Serengeti National Park, enjoy a picnic lunch and game drive until sunset. Dinner and overnight at Suenos de Africa Luxury Camp.

Day 9-10: Serengeti National park
Another 2 full days of game driving in Central Serengeti National Park. Dinner and overnight at Suenos de Africa Luxury Camp.

Day 11: Ngorongoro Crater
Explore Ngorongoro Crater with a game drive and viewing, then transfer by road to Baghayo Garden Suites for dinner and overnight stay.

Day 12: Departure
Enjoy Maasai cultural activities after breakfast, then transfer to Kilimanjaro Airport for departure.
