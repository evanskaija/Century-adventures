12 Days The best of South and Northern Tanzania Safari

12 Days Nyerere, Mikumi, Serengeti, Ngorongoro From $5,221

Tour Overview

This ultimate 12-day safari connects the wild, untouched landscapes of Southern Tanzania with the world-famous iconic parks of the North. Experience the massive elephants of Nyerere, the waterfalls of Mikumi, and the legendary migration of the Serengeti.

Itinerary Highlights

Day 1-3: Nyerere National Park (Selous)

Boat safaris and walking tours in Africa's largest protected area.

Day 4-6: Mikumi & Udzungwa

Explore the 'Little Serengeti' and trek the Sanje Waterfalls.

Day 7-10: Serengeti National Park

Witness the Great Migration and the 'Big Five' in action.

Day 11-12: Ngorongoro Crater & Departure

Descend into the world's largest volcanic caldera.
