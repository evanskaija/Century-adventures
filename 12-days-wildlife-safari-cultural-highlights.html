DAY-BY-DAY ITINERARY

Day 1-2: Arusha & West Kilimanjaro

Arrival and immersion into Maasai culture at the foot of Kilimanjaro.

  • Maasai cultural dance and story-telling
  • Spear throwing competition
  • Overnight at Kambi Ya Tembo

Day 3-5: Chemka, Tarangire & Manyara

Relax in natural hot springs before heading to the 'Home of Elephants'.

  • Swimming in Chemka Hot Springs
  • Game drives in Tarangire National Park
  • Tree-climbing lions of Lake Manyara

Day 6-9: Serengeti & Ngorongoro

The ultimate wildlife experience in the world's most famous crater and plains.

  • Great Migration tracking in Serengeti
  • Big Five sightings in Ngorongoro Crater
  • Visit to Olduvai Gorge

Day 10-12: Lake Natron & Departure

Witness the base of the holy mountain Ol Doinyo Lengai and thousands of flamingos.

  • Lake Natron flamingo viewing
  • Return to Arusha for your outbound flight
