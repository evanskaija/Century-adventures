10-Day Best of Tanzania

Honeymoon Safari

Are you dreaming of a truly unforgettable honeymoon experience? Look no further than the 10-Day Best of Tanzania Honeymoon Safari – All-Inclusive. This luxury honeymoon safari and beach package is designed to create lifelong memories for you and your partner.

Your journey begins with a thrilling wildlife safari in the renowned Tarangire Serengeti and Ngorongoro crater. After the excitement of the safari, it’s time to unwind and relax on the beautiful beaches of Zanzibar. Zanzibar is a tropical paradise with pristine white sandy beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Spend your days basking in the sun, swimming in the ocean, or indulging in spa treatments. The island also offers a rich cultural experience, with its blend of Arab, African, and European influences, which you can explore through visits to the historic Stone Town or local spice farms.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arusha-Arrival
Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport in Arusha and transfer to your accommodation at Gran Melia Arusha. Spend the rest of the day relaxing and acclimatizing to the surroundings.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park
After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park for a full day of game drives. Spot a variety of wildlife including elephants, giraffes, lions, and zebras. In the evening, check into Maramboi Tented Lodge for overnight stay.

Day 3: Lake Manyara National Park

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater

Day 5-6: Central Serengeti National Park

Day 7-9: Zanzibar Island

Day 10: Departure
