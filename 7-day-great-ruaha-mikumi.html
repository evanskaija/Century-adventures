7-Day Great Ruaha & Mikumi

National Park Safari

Embark on the adventure of a lifetime with our 7-Day Great Ruaha & Mikumi National Park Safari! Discover the stunning landscapes and fascinating wildlife of two of Tanzania’s most renowned national parks.

Our expert guides will lead you on game drives, bush walks and providing insider knowledge and helping you to capture unforgettable moments on camera. Relax in comfortable accommodations and enjoy delicious meals under the African sky, surrounded by the sounds of the bush.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Dar es Salaam
Welcome to Tanzania! Upon arrival at Julius Nyerere International Airport in Dar es Salaam, you will be transferred to your accommodation at Hotel Slipway. Take some time to relax and settle in after your journey. If time allows, you can explore the nearby Slipway area with its shops, restaurants, and beautiful views of the Indian Ocean.

Day 2: Dar es salaam to Dar es Salaam
After breakfast, transfer by road to Dar es Salaam. Enjoy lunch and dinner at the Dar es Salaam Sunset Hotel.

Day 3: Ruaha National park
Embark on full-day game drives in Ruaha National Park, exploring its diverse habitats and spotting large elephant herds.

Day 4: Morning walking & Game drive
Experience a thrilling morning walking safari with armed rangers, followed by afternoon game drives in Ruaha.

Day 5: Ruaha to Mikumi National park
Transfer from Ruaha to Mikumi National Park, arriving for dinner and rest at Mikumi Wildlife Camp.

Day 6: Mikumi National park
Enjoy a full day of wildlife viewing in Mikumi National Park, exploring the Mkata floodplains.

Day 7: Departure
Transfer by road back to Dar es Salaam or the airport for departure.
