Safari & Travel Activities

Choose your adventures: game viewing, mountain climbs, tropical beach sports, and immersive Maasai cultures.

Wildlife

?? Classic Game Drives

Track the Big Five from comfortable open-roof 4x4 safari vehicles. Experience morning and afternoon game drives led by professional local trackers.

Daily All National Parks
Luxury

?? Hot Air Balloon Safaris

Drift silently over the Serengeti plains at sunrise, observing wild herds from above. Finish with a gourmet champagne bush breakfast.

Sunrise Serengeti / Tarangire
Adventure

?? Guided Nature Walks

Walk through untamed wilderness accompanied by an armed ranger. Get closer to the smaller details of the bush and track animal prints.

2-4 Hours Ruaha / Selous
Predators

?? Leopard Tracking Quest

Join our highly specialized guides to locate and observe elusive leopards resting in acacia trees. Learn secrets of wild cat tracking.

Half Day Serengeti / Ngorongoro
Trekking

?? Mount Kilimanjaro Climbing

Trek to the roof of Africa, Uhuru Peak, standing at 5,895m. Climb via Machame, Marangu, or Lemosho routes supported by professional guides.

6-8 Days Kilimanjaro
Hiking

?? Mount Meru Trekking

Trek Tanzania's second-highest peak, Mount Meru (4,562m). Enjoy breathtaking views of Kilimanjaro and encounters with wildlife on the lower slopes.

3-4 Days Arusha National Park
Water Sport

?? Momella Lakes Canoeing

Paddle a stable canoe along the momella lakes. Glide close to flamingos, waterbucks, and watering giraffes, enjoying tranquil forest backdrops.

2-3 Hours Arusha National Park
Beaches

?? Marine Safaris & Snorkeling

Explore the beautiful coral reefs of Mnemba Atoll. Swim alongside sea turtles, dolphins, and hundreds of tropical fish species in crystal waters.

Half Day Zanzibar Islands
Sailing

? Traditional Dhow Sunset Sail

Sail on a traditional wooden dhow as the sun goes down. Sip refreshing Swahili juices, taste seafood snacks, and enjoy traditional island melodies.

3 Hours Stone Town / Nungwi
Spices

?? Spice Farm Guided Walking Tour

Walk through tropical spice farms. Smell, touch, and taste cloves, vanilla, nutmeg, cardamom, and exotic tropical fruits in their natural state.

3 Hours Kizimbani Village
Culture

?? Maasai Village Visits

Meet the iconic Maasai people, learn about their pastoral lifestyles, participate in traditional dances, and discover their beadwork craftsmanship.

2-3 Hours Ngorongoro / Serengeti
Cooking

?? Swahili Cooking Masterclass

Learn how to cook authentic Swahili cuisine with a local home chef. Prepare fresh coconut curry, pilau rice, and traditional spiced tea.

3-4 Hours Zanzibar / Dar es Salaam
Music

?? Traditional Drumming & Rhythms

Immerse yourself in Tanzanian music. Learn traditional drumming patterns and join in the high-energy dances of local tribal communities.

2 Hours Bagamoyo / Arusha
